# Promotion gate

This record is acquisition-only. Do not promote directly to active. A new
conditional pilot requires:

- [ ] All 2,058 sequence-set URLs, lengths, public dates, downloaded byte sizes,
      and checksums are pinned from ENA.
- [ ] Inputs are sampled before author species clustering and representative
      selection; the current PRJEB31003 representatives remain blocked.
- [ ] Author species clusters, representatives, taxonomy, trees, novelty labels,
      functional annotations, abundance profiles, geography, phenotype, and
      disease associations are absent from all grants.
- [ ] Hidden accession mappings remain evaluator-side and cannot be requested by
      the under-evaluation agent.
- [ ] Similarity thresholds, aligned-fraction thresholds, quality filters, and
      cluster representatives are varied in sensitivity analysis.
- [ ] Deterministic fixtures verify de novo cluster metrics without requiring
      literal recovery of author cluster IDs.
- [ ] Resource-bounded staging, leak scans, and a blinded joinability review pass.
- [ ] Conclusions are explicitly limited to the representative-sequence pilot,
      not the full raw-metagenome assembly or the paper's cohort associations.
- [ ] A blinded baseline reproduces deterministic clustering and representative
      selection under input-order changes and threshold sensitivity.
